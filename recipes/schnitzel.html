Chicken Schnitzel

Description

Tasty and easy to make chicken schnitzel

Ingredients

  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 tablespoons honey
  • 3 tablespoons Dijon mustard
  • 2 1/2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon crushed garlic
  • 3 cups bread crumbs
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • vegetable oil for frying
  • 12 boneless chicken breasts, halved and pounded thin
  • 1 lemon, cut into wedges

Steps

  1. Whisk eggs, honey, Dijon mustard, soy sauce and garlic in a bowl
  2. Mix bread crumbs, salt and pepper in a shallow bowl
  3. Heat 1/2 to 1 inch of begetable oil in a heavy skillet to 350F
  4. Put chicken slices in the egg mix and knead until fully coated
  5. Fry until no longer pink in center and browned on the outside, 3-4 minutes per side
  6. Serve with lemon wedges
