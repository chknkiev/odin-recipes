Creamy Jalapeno and Pulled Pork Quesadilla

Description

Spicy cream cheese, pulled pork and veggies in a quesadilla

Ingredients

  • 4 flour tortillas
  • 1/2 cup Spicy Jalapeno cream cheese
  • 1 package sweet & spicy pulled pork
  • 1/2 cup frozen corn, thawed
  • 1/2 cup chopped red pepper
  • 1/2 cup tomatillo salsa

Steps

  1. Spread cream cheese on tortillas
  2. Add toppings to one half of tortilla. Fold tortillas in half
  3. Heat large skillet on medium heat. Cook for 4-5 minutes turning after 2 minutes
  4. Serve with salsa
