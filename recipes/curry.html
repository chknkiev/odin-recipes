Potato Curry

Description

This is a great creamy and mild curry recipe

Ingredients

  • 3 Tablespoons Gee
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon tumeric
  • 1 teaspoon ground coriander
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1/2 teaspoon mustard seed
  • 1/2 teaspoon ground cayenne pepper
  • 6 medium potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 2 cups water
  • 1 cup yoghurt
  • 2/3 cup frozen green peas

Steps

  1. Heat the ghee in a skillet over medium heat
  2. Mix in the cumin, tumeric, oriander, salt, mustard seed and cayenne pepper
  3. Place potatoes in the skillet and stir to evenly coat with ghee. Cook for 10 minutes, stirring often
  4. Pour water into the skilet. Reduce heat to low and simmer for 30 minutes
  5. Mix yoghurt and peas into the saucepan. Continue cooking until heated through
